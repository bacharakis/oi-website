Open Innovation Team, Mozilla

Openness, community, collaboration …. These values are the core of open innovation, which is how Mozilla delivers great products and technologies that keep the Internet open and accessible to all.

Projects

The Open Innovation team directly manages several product, technology and data focused initiatives and programs. We also help Mozilla connect with the expertise, creativity, capacity and influence of existing and new communities who align with Mozilla’s mission.

Open Innovation and Mozilla’s Communities

We maintain tools and systems like the Community Portal and Mozilla’s Community Participation Guidelines (CPG), which facilitate meaningful, inclusive experiences for everyone who wants to support Mozilla’s mission by volunteering their time, joining the community, or running local events or activities.

Interested in volunteering? The Open Innovation team also manages and supports numerous volunteer opportunities around Mozilla. No matter where you live or what your skills and interests might be -- from contributing code to localizing content into your language -- there’s a place waiting for you in one of Mozilla’s contributor communities!

You can join the conversation through Discourse or get great contribution opportunities straight to your inbox by subscribing here.
